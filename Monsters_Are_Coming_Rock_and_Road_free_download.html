About This Game

**Monsters Are Coming! Rock & Road** is a clever hybrid that blends the **action-roguelite** gameplay of Survivor-likes with classic **Tower Defense** and **City Builder** elements. You take on the role of a lone hero tasked with protecting an **ever-moving city on wheels** as it travels a dangerous road toward the Ark. Fight relentless hordes of monsters while managing your resources to fortify your rolling fortress.

Gather **wood** to power your city's defenses, **stone** for repairs, and **gold** to purchase new districts and upgrades in sheltered checkpoints. Strategic placement of your defensive structures (towers, outposts, etc.) is key, as your city expands with every upgrade you choose. Balance the risk of venturing out for resources against the reward of permanent progression and new build options. If the hero falls, a new one spawns, but if the city is destroyed, the run is over.
